Test NameStart charge while other port is charging another EV with different battery voltage topologies.
Test IdentifierITP-02-05-003
Test TypeSimultaneous Single Dispenser Charging (Multiple EVs required)
Test CategoryAdvanced Charging Tests
Test PurposeTo ensure EVSE can seamlessly start charge and deliver power to two EVs with different battery voltage topologies at the same time.
Observed MetricsVoltage [V(DC)], EVSE UI Behavior, Power split between ports
Intended MRECs/Errors--
Other Possible MRECs--
Test ConditionsEV Initial SoC<75%
EV SoC Charge Limit100%
HLC Protocol/sDIN SPEC 70121, ISO 15118-2, ISO 15118-20
Involved System/sEV, EVSE
Steps1. Set EVSE authentication option to Any.
2. Plug in EV1 with <500V battery topology to port 1 of EVSE, authorize charge, and observe power transfer.
3. Wait for 2 min.
4. Plug in EV2 with >500V battery topology to port 2 of EVSE, authorize charge, and observe power transfer.
5. Wait for 2 min and observe EVSE UI.
6. Manually terminate the charge session 2-3 minutes into power transfer.
7. Unplug both EVs from their respective EVSE ports.
